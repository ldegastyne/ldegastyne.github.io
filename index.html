I am a senior at Virginia Tech studying Sociology (with a concentration in Social Inequality) and Professional & Technical Writing. This site was created for a specific project in ENGL 3844 Writing & Digital Media. My group's project is to advocate the cause of anti-sex trafficking specifically in Atlanta, GA because it is the #1 city in the United States for sex trafficking. By raising awareness about the reality of sex trafficking and providing practical prevention techniques through blog posts on Medium, a video, and a podcast (all located below on this site), we hope to make a difference in that city.
